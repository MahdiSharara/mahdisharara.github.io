About Me

I am a 6G/AI Research Scientist at the CTO Office of VIAVI Solutions, where I focus on designing cutting-edge machine learning algorithms for radio and computing resource allocation in Open Radio Access Networks (O-RAN).

My research aims to optimize the performance and efficiency of future mobile networks by leveraging advanced techniques in deep learning, reinforcement learning, and large language models. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical implementation in next-generation telecommunications.

I hold a PhD in Telecommunications from Université Paris-Saclay (2023), where I developed novel techniques for radio and computing resource allocation in 5G networks.
